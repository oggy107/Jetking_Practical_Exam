Q -> Demonstrate on How to create and setup Metamask account and
wallet?

1. Open a browser of your choice
2. search "metamask" and click on first link
3. click on download button. This will start downloading metamask extension for your browser.
4. After download is complete click on "create a new wallet button".
5. Follow the procedures on your screen.
6. setup a new password for your wallet.
7. Note down recovery phrase somewhere safe. This will be used to recover your lost metamask account
8. Confirm your recovery phrase.
9. After completing all steps you will get promted with "Wallet creatation successfull" message.
10. Now you can start using metamask wallet to connect with DAPPS and send transactions.
